Shadow Chase

Local browser prototype for up to 4 players. One player can use touch, mouse drag, or WASD. Other players can be AI or keyboard controlled. This version is tuned for quick testing and GitHub Pages deployment.

Players and characters

Round settings

Core rule
If a player enters your light cone and they are not facing toward you, you become the chaser.
Chaser bonus
Slight speed boost plus a blinking gold ring.
Hunted bonus
Larger light radius plus red warning pulse.
Win condition
Catch the hunted player before time runs out.
Mobile
Player 1 can move with the touch pad or by dragging directly on the arena.
